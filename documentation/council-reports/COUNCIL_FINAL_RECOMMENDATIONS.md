## 🚨 **CRITICAL FINDINGS REQUIRING IMMEDIATE ATTENTION**

### 1. **REFERENCE GENE INSTABILITY - PRIORITY 1**
- **Issue:** GAPDH shows significant instability across disease groups (p < 0.001)
- **Impact:** Questions reliability of all quantification results
- **Solution:** Implement multi-gene reference panel validation (GAPDH + β-actin + U6 snRNA)
- **Timeline:** Must be addressed before manuscript submission

### 2. **PERFECT CLASSIFICATION CONCERN - PRIORITY 1**
- **Issue:** AUC = 1.000 suggests potential overfitting
- **Impact:** Results may not generalize to new populations
- **Solution:** External validation cohort (n ≥ 150) and regularization methods
- **Timeline:** Required for high-impact journal submission

### 3. **STATISTICAL ROBUSTNESS - PRIORITY 2**
- **Issue:** Missing nested cross-validation and permutation testing
- **Impact:** May have data leakage and inflated performance metrics
- **Solution:** Implement nested CV and bootstrap validation
- **Timeline:** 2-3 weeks additional analysis
